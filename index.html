FAQ

How does this tool work? +
NaturalTTS uses Piper, a neural speech synthesis system that converts text into speech. Everything runs directly in your browser.
Is there a text limit? +
Yes, you can convert up to 5,000 characters at once (approximately 750-1000 words).
What file format is the audio downloaded in? +
MP3 format, which is compatible with most devices and media players.
Is my data private? +
Yes, completely. All processing happens in your browser, and no data is sent to any server.
