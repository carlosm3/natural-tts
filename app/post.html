Introducing NaturalTTS v2.3: Smoother Voices and New Languages

May 10, 2025 â€¢ 5 min read

Our latest update brings significant improvements to voice quality and adds support for three new languages. These enhancements are part of our ongoing commitment to providing the most natural-sounding text-to-speech experience directly in your browser.

What's New in v2.3

The NaturalTTS v2.3 release focuses on refining voice quality and expanding language support. Here are the key improvements:

  • Enhanced prosody modeling for more natural intonation patterns
  • Improved handling of punctuation and pauses
  • Better pronunciation of loan words and foreign names
  • Reduced latency for faster speech generation
  • Support for three new languages: Dutch, Polish, and Swedish

Technical Improvements

Behind the scenes, we've made several technical improvements to make NaturalTTS even better:

Performance Optimizations

We've optimized the WebAssembly code to reduce the processing time by approximately 30%. This results in noticeably faster speech generation, especially for longer texts.

Processing time reduced from ~70ms to ~47ms per character

The voice model architecture has been refined to better capture the nuances of natural speech. We've integrated an improved attention mechanism that helps the model focus on the right phonetic contexts, resulting in more human-like prosody and fewer pronunciation errors.

Getting Started with v2.3

To start using the new version, simply refresh your browser or clear your cache if you're still seeing the previous version. The update is automatically applied, with no need to download or install anything.

Using the New Languages

To use one of the new languages, select it from the language dropdown menu in the main interface. The voices will be automatically loaded when you select the language.

What's Next?

We continue to work on enhancing NaturalTTS with more features and improvements. Here's what you can expect in upcoming releases:

  • Voice customization options for adjusting speech characteristics
  • Support for SSML (Speech Synthesis Markup Language) for fine-grained control
  • Additional language support including Arabic, Korean, and Portuguese
  • More voice options for existing languages
  • Enhanced handling of complex technical terminology

We value your feedback and are continuously working to improve NaturalTTS based on user suggestions. If you have any comments or encounter any issues with the new release, please don't hesitate to reach out via our contact form.

Thank you for using NaturalTTS. We're excited to continue this journey of making high-quality speech synthesis accessible to everyone.
