Frequently Asked Questions

Technical details about our speech synthesis engine

How does this tool work? +

NaturalTTS uses Piper, a neural speech synthesis system that converts text into speech through a spectrogram-based autoregressive model. Everything runs directly in your browser using WebAssembly technology.

The system processes your text in four stages:

  1. Text normalization and phoneme conversion
  2. Linguistic feature extraction
  3. Neural network speech prediction
  4. Audio waveform generation
$ piper --model en_US_female.onnx --output_file speech.wav
Is there a text limit? +

Yes, you can convert up to 5,000 characters at once (approximately 750-1000 words). This limit helps ensure optimal performance in the browser environment.

For longer texts, we recommend breaking them into smaller chunks and processing them sequentially. The memory footprint increases linearly with text length.

Is my data private? +

Yes, completely. All processing happens locally in your browser, and no data is sent to any server. Your text never leaves your device, ensuring 100% privacy and security.
