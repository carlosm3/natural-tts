Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our text-to-speech tool

How does this tool work? +

NaturalTTS uses Piper, a state-of-the-art neural speech synthesis system that converts text into speech. Everything runs directly in your browser using WebAssembly technology, which means your text never leaves your device.

The system processes your text, analyzes the linguistic features, and generates natural-sounding speech that can be played back instantly or downloaded as an MP3 file.

Is there a text limit? +

Yes, you can convert up to 5,000 characters at once (approximately 750-1000 words). This limit helps ensure optimal performance in the browser environment.

For longer texts, we recommend breaking them into smaller chunks and processing them sequentially.

What file format is the audio downloaded in? +

All generated audio is available in MP3 format, which offers excellent audio quality with small file sizes. MP3 files are compatible with virtually all devices and media players, making them ideal for a wide range of applications.

Is my data private? +

Yes, completely. All processing happens locally in your browser, and no data is sent to any server. Your text never leaves your device, ensuring 100% privacy and security.

This makes NaturalTTS an excellent choice for sensitive content or situations where data privacy is a priority.

Can I use the generated audio for commercial purposes? +

Yes, you can use the generated audio for personal and commercial projects. Since the processing is done locally on your device, you own the output you create.

This makes NaturalTTS perfect for creating content for YouTube videos, podcasts, e-learning materials, and other media projects.
